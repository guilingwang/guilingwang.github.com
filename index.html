Guiling Wang is the associate professor of Computer Science at Beijing Key Laboratory on Integration and Analysis of Large-scale Stream Data, North China University of Technology, with a focus on Streaming Data Integration and Analysis, Big Data as A Service, Cloud Computing and Service Computing. From 2007-2011 she is an assistant professor and post doctor in Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences (ICT, CAS). She obtained her Ph.D. in DCST of Tsinghua University in 2007. She act as the principal investigator (PI) of National Natural Science Foundation of China(2010-2012), Post-Doctor Science Foundation of China in 2008, and a Changfeng Alliance SOA Strategic Study Project in 2008. She also acted as co-PI or main member of other NSFC,863,and collaboration project from industry. She has published some 30 scientific papers as conference- and journal- contributions. She has writen 3 academic books (2nd author and 3rd,4th author). She has been authorized 2 patents. She is the PC member of ICSOC'12-15, SOCA'09-10, PCS WISE Workshop'11-15, and EDOC SCDI'12 workshop etc.
